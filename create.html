ABOUT US


Smitha Computer Education Center is a Franchise of The Rastriya Computer Saksharatha Samithi.This Institute was found in the year 2003 & is Directed By G.T Subramanya Bhat.

Over 8000+ students have passed out from this Institute with a verified Certificate.Students are given trainings for teaching and other DTP jobs and have obtained jobs in good enterprises and Schools with high Salary.
